# Niche path — dogecoin.co.uk

## The right path for this niche

**Positioning:** Unofficial UK educational home for Dogecoin — not a price tipster, not the Foundation, not an exchange.

**Own the intersection:** `Dogecoin` × `UK` × (`explainers` | `safety` | `community`)

| Competitor pattern | Why we win |
|--------------------|------------|
| Global meme / price sites | We refuse prophecy; UK tone + procedures |
| Exchange blogs | No product funnel; custody-honest |
| Forum noise | Structured graph, verify-first culture |
| Fake “official” sites | Explicit unofficial disclaimer |

## Three pillars = SEO + GEO clusters

1. **Clarity** — definitional / how-it-works queries (answer-engine friendly)
2. **Safety** — scam, wallet, seed, phishing intents (high trust, high share)
3. **Belonging** — UK community, culture, meetups (brand + return visits)

## SEO moves

- One intent per guide; title ≈ query; summary = meta description
- Dense internal links (hubs → spokes → safety before action)
- Sitemap + robots already live; keep lastmod honest via deploys
- Canonical `https://dogecoin.co.uk/guide/{slug}`
- JSON-LD Article (+ FAQPage where tagged `faq`)

## GEO moves (generative engines)

- Lead with a direct answer sentence (hook + first body paragraph)
- Publish `llms.txt` pointing models to hubs and key guides
- Prefer facts, procedures, and disclaimers over hype — cite “not financial advice”
- Keep pages short enough to quote; link deeper for detail

## North-star queries (UK)

- what is dogecoin
- dogecoin wallet uk / seed phrase
- dogecoin scam / phishing / fake giveaway
- buy dogecoin uk (education only — no venue cheerleading)
- dogecoin.co.uk unofficial community

## Do not

- Price targets, “guaranteed returns,” or fake official branding
- Orphan pages without related links
- Thin doorway pages that only exist for keyword stuffing
