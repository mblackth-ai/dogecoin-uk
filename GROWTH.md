# dogecoin.co.uk — Growth Doctrine

## Why people land here (psychology)

1. **Domain authority heuristic** — `.co.uk` feels local and “official enough,” lowering threat detection before a word is read.
2. **Belonging hunger** — DOGE culture is identity theatre; UK visitors want *their* corner of the tribe.
3. **Fear of looking stupid** — people search quietly for plain English so they can rejoin the meme with confidence.
4. **Scam anxiety** — crypto search intent is half curiosity, half “don’t get wrecked.”
5. **Curiosity gap** — cultural gravity of Dogecoin pulls clicks; unfinished mental models keep them scrolling.
6. **Certainty substitution** — when markets are noisy, calm structured pages feel like shelter.
7. **Reciprocity / kindness brand** — Dogecoin’s soft power is generosity; a useful free guide triggers obligation to return.

## Three pillars of success

| Pillar | Promise | Psychological lever | Site job |
|--------|---------|---------------------|----------|
| **Clarity** | Understand DOGE without jargon | Cognitive ease + competence | Explainers, glossaries, how-it-works |
| **Safety** | Don’t get wrecked in the UK context | Anxiety reduction + control | Checklists, phishing, custody, red flags |
| **Belonging** | You’re not alone here | Identity + social proof | Community pulse, UK angle, culture, myths |

## Why this works doing what it does

- **Search → shelter loop**: land for one answer, stay for the adjacent unanswered question (internal links = curiosity bridges).
- **Pillar triangulation**: every page reinforces Clarity, Safety, or Belonging — never random filler.
- **Surface area compounding**: more interlinked pages = more entry doors + longer sessions + stronger topical authority.
- **Anti-hype trust**: refusing price calls dissolves the skeptic filter and increases shareability among cautious UK users.
- **Self-growing graph**: each new page must link out *and* get linked in — the site becomes a web, not a brochure.

## Growth rule (heartbeat)

Every pass: add pages, deepen cross-links, strengthen hubs, redeploy. No orphan pages. No dead ends.
